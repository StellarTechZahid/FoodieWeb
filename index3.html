Cooking Tips & Tricks

Kitchen Essentials

Every Pakistani kitchen should have these essential tools and ingredients to prepare authentic dishes with ease.

  • A good quality pressure cooker for quick cooking of lentils and meat
  • Cast iron or heavy-bottomed karahi (wok) for authentic taste
  • Wooden spoons to avoid scratching cookware
  • Spice box (masala dabba) with essential spices
Read full guide →

Spice Blending Tips

Master the art of blending spices for perfect Pakistani flavors every time.

  • Toast whole spices before grinding to enhance flavor
  • Store ground spices in airtight containers away from direct sunlight
  • Add garam masala at the end of cooking for maximum aroma
  • Balance flavors with the right proportion of sweet, sour, and savory spices

Our Story

FoodieApp was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to preserve and share authentic Pakistani recipes while making them accessible to everyone, regardless of their cooking experience.

What started as a small recipe blog has grown into a comprehensive platform featuring hundreds of recipes, cooking videos, and a food delivery service that brings authentic flavors right to your doorstep.

Our team of passionate chefs and food enthusiasts is dedicated to testing and perfecting every recipe before it reaches you.
